How Are We Doing This?

All major blockchains build brides with Ethereum. We aggregate all these bridges to make one single interconnected network. All bridge specific information is abstracted away.

OUR VISION

Our Vision. Why are we building this?

We envision a future where the underlying details of the blockchain will be abstracted away. Users & developers will get one single interface to connect with the entire blockchain ecosystem. Liquidity locked up across multiple networks will be connected.

Why did we start building this? We were frustrated to be forced to pick up one single blockchain to build on.

ELECTRON TOKEN

The Electron Token

Electron token is not only our native crypto-currency, it is also Electron protocol's first use case. Electron is the world's first blockchain agnostic token. You can transact this token on any blockchain, without having to wrap or unwrap it.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQ'S

Is Electron a new blockchain?

No, it is not. Introducing a new blockchain will create new security risks, and will be very hard to get off the ground. Electron protocol is a combination of smart contracts, relayers, and most importantly, blockchain bridges. 

Is Electron Protocol decentralized?

We borrow from the security of the blockchains we interconnect. We do not require new trust assumptions. As long as the inter-connected blockchains stay true, Electron stay's true.

How does Electron Token Work?

Electron token is natively present on every blockchain we interconnect. When the Electron token is transferred from one blockchain to another, the Electron smart contracts consume the cross-chain tokens and issue the native token instead, solving the wrapping problem
